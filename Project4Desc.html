Project #4 Description

For this project, I didn't have a specific visual look I was going for with any preset. Instead I was trying more to create a series of presets that would look far out and colorful!

For preset #1, I chose to go with a mint blue color and straight lines to portray more of a "frozen electric shock" sort of look. I think I suceeded in this and I managed to acheive the look I was going for by increasing the search distance and minimizing the pixel distance. I think it turned out pretty interesting.

For Preset #2, I chose a bright green color that gives the design a very chemical/organic look. This design is in my opinion the least interesting of the lot, simply because it isn't varied that much despite having many modifiers applied to its different values.

Preset #3 consists of a small pixel scale, a modified 'PI_4' value, and a small search value. Accompanied by an orange/brown color, I find it comes together to create an almost organic leather looking design.

Preset #4 is my favorite of them all, simply because I always find that red on black is a fun and interesting color contrast. this preset shares a number of values from preset #1. The major difference is that it has a much larger pixel scale and a slightly shorter search distance when compared with the search distance of the first preset.
